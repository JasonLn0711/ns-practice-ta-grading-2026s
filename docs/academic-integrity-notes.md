# Academic Integrity Notes

## Principle

Do not accuse from memory. Preserve evidence, compare carefully, and escalate suspicious cases to the instructor.

## What To Log

- student identifiers
- files compared
- similarity concern
- objective evidence
- whether the same mistake / output / structure appears in multiple submissions
- action taken

## What Not To Do

- Do not decide a misconduct penalty alone unless the instructor has delegated that authority.
- Do not write sensitive suspicion notes in daily planning files.
- Do not share raw student work outside the grading context.

## Escalation Template

```text
homework:
students:
files:
concern:
evidence:
recommended next step:
instructor decision:
```
